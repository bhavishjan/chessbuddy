Features

ChessBuddy is now more than a landing page.

The site still introduces the product cleanly, but it now also includes a real chess surface for local two-sided play and customization.

01

Fully legal local play

Every move is validated so you can only make legal moves, including castling, en passant, and promotion.

02

Play both sides

Use one board for local practice, move for White and Black, and flip the orientation whenever you want.

03

Theme and board control

Change the board colors, choose a board variation, and switch between multiple piece style presets.

04

Game-state feedback

Track move history, captures, turn status, check states, and game-end conditions in the side panel.
